10 Reasons Why You Should Charter A Bus Around Palo Alto

Before you head to Palo Alto, consider renting a charter bus to take you around the city. Charter buses are safe, reliable solutions for anyone struggling to find an affordable ride through Silicon Valley. While there are a myriad of companies out there for you to choose from, San Francisco Charter Bus Company is the only one that provides extraordinary service on a consistent basis. Having serviced thousands of customers in the Bay Area, we know exactly how to satisfy your transportation needs.

Not sure if motorcoach is the right solution to your transportation needs? Check out our top 10 reasons you should charter a bus in Palo Alto:

1. YOU'LL SAVE ON AIRFARE & CAR RENTAL FEES.

Avoid expensive airfare and hidden rental car fees when you book a motorcoach with us. Even if you come from outside cities like San Mateo or Fremont, you'll often pay less per person when you book a bus.

2. YOUR BUS SCHEDULE IS COMPLETELY PERSONALIZED TO YOU.

Taking public transportation means waiting in lines, traveling only along pre-set routes, and working around inflexible schedules. You’re able to create your own itinerary when you rent motorcoach, so you can arrive on time to any event, and plan as many stops as you’d like along the way.

3. STYLE AND COMFORT COME STANDARD

Upon request, luxury motorcoaches may be equipped with a number of modern amenities, including reclining seats, TV monitors, a DVD player, a restroom, WiFi, power outlets, and more. With overhead bins above the seats and storage bays below the bus, your luggage will be safe and out of the way during your trip.

4. TRAVELING BY CHARTER BUS MEANS TRAVELING TOGETHER.

Large groups need to ride together for safety and efficiency. Carpooling and ride-share apps like Uber can divide groups of passengers into multiple vehicles, but a charter bus rental ensures that nobody arrives late, gets lost, or fails to make it.

To arrive on time while socializing with your group, call our reservation specialists at 650-262-7063. We'll provide you with a free quote and can answer any questions you may have about chartering a bus.

5. YOUR DRIVER WILL DEAL WITH TRAFFIC AND PARKING.

Let your driver navigate the streets of Palo Alto while you enjoy the city. He or she will plot the best route to get you to your destination promptly and safely. Once you arrive at your destination, your driver finds a parking spot while you explore Palo Alto. Focus on having fun, not logistics, with your own charter bus.

6. RELAXATION AND TEAM BONDING COMES FIRST.

Unlike traveling by car, bus transportation allows you to kick back and spend time with your friends, family, and colleagues. Instead of worrying about rush hour traffic on Highway 101 or looking up directions to your next stop, play games and chat with the people you're traveling with.

7. YOU'LL HAVE A KNOWLEDGEABLE DRIVER.

We only work with experienced, professional drivers. If you need suggestions for restaurants or activities during your stay, don't hesitate to ask your driver. He or she will be available to answer your questions and provide you with insider tips for traveling in Palo Alto.

8. BUSINESS PROFESSIONALS CAN STAY CONNECTED.

Just because you're traveling to a trade show or conference doesn't mean you and your colleagues can't be productive. Stay connected with onboard WiFi and power outlets to conduct meetings, message clients, and receive updates on the road.

9. SAFETY IS OUR TOP PRIORITY.

Because we only charter buses that are compliant with California's Department of Transportation safety standards, we've earned a reputation of putting our customers' well-being first. With San Francisco Charter Bus Company, you can travel with confidence and assurance.

10. YOUR LUGGAGE AND EQUIPMENT STAY WITH YOU

When traveling by air, your luggage and equipment is loaded separately and sometimes results in lost belongings. Even when carpooling, luggage can get separated into multiple vehicles, making it difficult to keep track of your items. On a motorcoach, you'll have plenty of storage with the luggage bays beneath the bus and overhead bins above the seats. Keep all of your belongings safe on the bus until you're ready to unload them when you arrive in Palo Alto.
