Private Bus Rentals in San Francisco

Thinking about planning a personal trip to San Francisco? Book with San Francisco Charter Bus Company for easy transportation to every stop on your itinerary. We service private events of all sizes, helping families, friends, church groups, and more travel through San Francisco with ease. Call our 24-hour reservation specialists at 415-404-6456​ to learn more about our private group transportation services!

Bus Options for Every Group

San Francisco Charter Bus Company charters a variety of motorcoaches, from minibuses capable of holding 18, 20, or 25 passengers to full-sized charter buses designed for groups of 50 or more. With such an extensive selection, we’re always able to find a bus that fits your group perfectly. In need of multiple buses to accommodate everyone? We’ll make sure you’re paired with the perfect fleet of buses for your group’s unique needs. Often, booking a bus for your family results in a lower travel cost per person than flying. Leave the transportation logistics to us while you focus on what’s important: enjoying your time with your family.

Comfortable Amenities for All Passengers

If you find your group stuck in San Francisco traffic, your bus will have plenty of amenities to help pass the time. Enjoy WiFi access and power outlets for endless entertainment, or put in your group’s favorite DVD. You can also take the opportunity to explore some of San Francisco’s famous landmarks right from your bus with panoramic windows.Family Reunion Travel in The Golden City

Bring your family into San Francisco for an unforgettable trip with the help of a bus rental from San Francisco Charter Bus Company. Book a private minibus to allow your family to travel as a unit to your favorite destinations in the city, from Fisherman’s Wharf to Golden Gate Park to any of the city’s countless museums and restaurants.

With a private bus rental, your family won’t have to worry about splitting into multiple cars and figuring out who will drive during the trip. Let a professional navigate you through the city’s congested streets while you enjoy your time with your family.

Simple Youth Group Transportation

Planning a trip to the Bay Area with your youth group? Rent a charter bus to guarantee safe and comfortable travels for every member of your church. We’ll match you with a bus tailored to your group’s requests for an enjoyable and stress-free trip. Instead of separating into multiple cars, your entire group can enjoy their time together on one vehicle while a professional driver handles all trip navigation. We’ll make sure you arrive on time to every destination, whether you’re spending a day exploring multiple stops in the city or heading out to a remote location for a weekend retreat.

Explore the Great Outdoors

With beautiful weather year-round and plenty of getaway spots for outdoor-lovers, there’s no shortage of breathtaking views to explore in and around San Francisco. We’ll take you to Golden Gate Park for an afternoon stroll, Ocean Beach for a day of lounging in the sun, or out to Mt. Diablo for a short hike filled with breathtaking views. Your driver will be ready to bring you back into the city when the day’s wrapping up, saving you the time and energy of waiting for public transportation or driving every member of your group back into town.

Visit the Annual Pride Parade and Festival

As the largest gathering of LGBT people and allies in the nation, San Francisco’s annual Pride Festival is a memorable experience for all visitors. Whether you need help getting into town or you’re looking for a reliable way to shuttle passengers around the city, San Francisco Charter Bus Company can pair you with a compact minibus just for your group, perfect for city travel before, during, and after the parade and festival. We’ll take your mind off of transportation worries so you can focus on enjoying the event to the fullest!
