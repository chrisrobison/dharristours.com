10 Reasons Why You Should Charter A Bus Around Fremont

Fremont is one of many San Francisco Bay Area cities bustling with businesses, entertainment, and culture. As is the case with many cities in the Bay Area, you may find it difficult to maneuver in Fremont. One way to maximize your travels is by renting a charter bus through San Francisco Charter Bus Company.

If you’re not sure what the benefits of utilizing a motorcoach are, check out our top 10 reasons to charter a bus in Fremont:

1. YOU'LL SAVE ON AIRFARE & CAR RENTAL FEES

Airplanes are one of the most expensive forms of transportation available, and car rentals can be tough to manage. You may be surprised to find that the group rates you pay for a bus rental are typically lower than what you typically pay for flights or car rentals.

2. THE BUS ROUTES AND SCHEDULES REVOLVE AROUND YOU

Charter buses travel according to your schedule and itinerary. Instead of worrying about when and where your bus will arrive when riding on BART’s pre-determined transit system, set your own schedule and choose your own pickup and drop-off points.

3. STYLE AND COMFORT COME STANDARD

San Francisco Charter Bus Company only books buses that are the most modern and technologically advanced. We give our customers the option to request amenities that improve the travel experience, including WiFi, electrical outlets, air conditioning, legroom, and reclining seats.

4. YOUR GROUP CAN TRAVEL TOGETHER

Mitigate the stress of separation from your group by avoiding public transit or carpools and booking a private coach. This way, you’re able to enjoy an intimate group environment in which everyone is in the same vehicle.

5. YOUR DRIVER WILL HANDLE THE HASSLE OF TRAFFIC AND PARKING

Sitting in traffic, navigating the streets, and finding parking are now a thing of the past. A private coach comes equipped with a driver who is responsible for your journey, from start to finish. While you enjoy time with your friends and family, relax, and take in the sights, your own personal chauffeur is handling the twists and turns of the road.

6. YOU CAN RELAX AND TAKE IN THE SIGHTS

Fremont is one of the most beautiful cities in the Bay. Don't miss out on the architecture, landscape, and scenery of the city because you're stuck staring at the road. Take a look around  while your driver focuses on the road.

7. YOU'LL HAVE A KNOWLEDGEABLE DRIVER

All drivers are assigned to areas they have a thorough knowledge of. Your driver will be so familiar with the area that he may be able to give you some inside information about the city, and limit the amount of extra time you spend sitting in traffic. Have a conversation, and you may find out some interesting tidbits about Fremont.

8. BUSINESS PROFESSIONALS CAN STAY CONNECTED

Staying in contact with your team and getting work done on the road has never been easier. If you request a minibus with WiFi and power outlets, you can connect to the internet and keep all of your devices running with ease. Don't use your time on the road as an excuse to fall behind, keep work moving with your premium amenities

9. SAFETY IS OUR TOP PRIORITY

It's our responsibility to keep you safe on the roads. All of the vehicles we work with are inspected by the Department of Transportation, and every driver is vetted long before they get behind the wheel. Trust San Francisco Charter Bus Company to protect you during your travels.

10. YOUR LUGGAGE AND EQUIPMENT STAY WITH YOU

Keep all of your luggage and equipment within reach by renting a minibus. Minibuses may be equipped with storage space overhead and underneath the cabin, so you have ample room to keep track of your possessions.
