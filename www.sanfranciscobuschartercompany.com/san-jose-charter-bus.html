About San Jose Charter Bus Company

San Jose, California

San Jose Charter Bus Company has everything you need for smooth transportation through San Jose and the surrounding areas. We’ve gained experience in providing transportation for a variety of trip types, so you can be sure that we’ll get you exactly where you need to be. No matter what type of bus your group needs, we’ll be your source for reliable bus rentals to the Municipal Rose Garden, the Children’s Discovery Museum, and more.

Choose from vehicle options such as minibuses and charter buses of multiple sizes, making it easy to accommodate all groups. Amenities include plush reclining seats, WiFi, power outlets, on-board restrooms, and more to make your trip enjoyable and comfortable. Not sure which bus is right for you? One of our reservation specialists will make sure you’re paired with the right model when you call us at 408-618-5737.

Group Events in San Jose

In need of transportation for an upcoming group event? San Jose Charter Bus Company services wedding guests, corporate travelers, and more during large events in San Jose. Bring your passengers in from other locations such as Fremont, San Mateo, Oakland, and more in the Bay Area. Once you’re here, we’ll find a minibus to act as your personal shuttle service at your event, ensuring that all guests travel seamlessly between your hotel and event space.

Sporting Event Travel

Whether you’re headed to support San Francisco’s 49ers or Giants or staying in town to see one of San Jose’s local teams, we’ll make sure you travel to all sporting events with ease. Book a minibus to transport your band of fans to the game and store all of your tailgating equipment right on board. Win or lose, you can celebrate throughout the match and enjoy safe, guaranteed transportation back home.

Have a team in need of transportation? We’ll put together a fleet to accommodate all players, staff members, coaches, and guests on the way to your next away game. With plenty of storage available, you can keep all equipment and luggage packed away and enjoy clean, clutter-free aisles during your trip.

Field Trips in the Bay Area

Take your students to local field trip stops such as the Children’s Discovery Museum or the Fujitsu Planetarium in Cupertino. Instead of using an old, uncomfortable school bus, book a private bus from San Jose Charter Bus Company to make sure that everyone enjoys the journey to your field trip destination. Students can watch videos on your bus’s monitors while chaperones and teachers can relax with reclining seats and WiFi. We’ll find an experienced driver for your group who will transport everyone safely around town and back to school when your trip is finished.
