35 Passenger Minibus Rental

Groups who rent the 35-passenger motor coach will enjoy the luxury amenities of a charter bus with the intimate feel of a minibus. Instead of coordinating carpool rides or public transportation, you can focus on spending quality time together as a group. Partner with DHarris Tours to book safe, reliable transportation for your upcoming trip.

More about the 35 Passenger Mini Bus

Choose from modern amenities such as:

  • Climate-controlled cabin
  • Cushioned, reclining seats
  • TV monitor and DVD player
  • Luggage storage
  • AM/FM radio
  • PA system
  • WiFi and power outlets

Discuss your trip’s details with our friendly reservation specialists today, and let them know which amenities your group requires. We’ll provide you with a free, no-obligation quote and a simple booking process when you call us 24/7 at (415) 902-8542. Whether you’re traveling for a corporate conference, wedding, sports tournament, church retreat, or other event, we’re looking forward to planning the perfect bus trip with you!

*For the safety of your passengers, we provide a licensed, professional bus driver with every rental at no additional cost to you. Unfortunately, we are not authorized to dispatch buses without drivers.
