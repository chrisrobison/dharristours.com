Palo Alto Group Transportation Made Simple

California’s Bay Area is full of interesting places to see and explore. Palo Alto is just one of those many intriguing spots in the locale, and if you’re hoping to organize group transportation out of Palo Alto, CA, DHarris Tours is your premier option for safe and stylish charter buses and minibuses.

Perhaps you need elegant wedding shuttles for your big day or a reliable, comfortable charter bus for far-away field trips to Disneyland. The ride is always worth it when you choose from our vast network of clean and trustworthy buses. The rider experience becomes even more enjoyable when you factor in the various amenities available to add to your vehicle. Perks such as onboard lavatories, free WiFi, and premium seats with extra legroom are all features that will make each rider feel right at home even when on wheels.

The process is simple: Call us any day at any time at 650-300-5388, tell us what you’re hoping to find, and let us work our magic. We’ll get you an affordable Palo Alto bus quote within minutes, so you can circle back with your team if needed or book right away—the choice is all yours.

Luxury Amenities for Very Important Passengers

Did you think VIP stood for Very Important Person? Think again. With us, it refers to Very Important Passengers, because we are committed to wowing every group we have the pleasure of servicing.

A group of business executives, for example, will enjoy the professional airport shuttles you’ve arranged to pick them up at Mineta San Jose International Airport so they don’t have to worry about navigating unfamiliar streets to get to Silicon Valley. Instead, they’ll get to relax on the ride, enjoying leather seats and plenty of space to place their luggage. Just ask about the sleek interiors we can include on the 18-passenger minibuses we charter.

Similarly, college tours are made simple with the thoughtful organization that comes with a 30-passenger minibus or 56-passenger charter (depending on the size of your group). You can gather a group of students and parents to tour the many universities in California, starting, of course, with Stanford University. When you book a charter for academic tours, just let your driver know your itinerary, and you’re all set! Your driver will get you to each destination safely, so all you have to worry about is what matters: the pros and cons of each college. Add reclining seats for naps and plug into personal power outlets to make sure your naptime music never stops.

The same goes for sports teams on the road to victory—trade out the traditional school bus for something much more spacious for long travel days. Show your competition you mean business with a large charter bus equipped with countless bells and whistles and storage galore.

Wedding Shuttles & Church Group Transportation

Planning your Bay Area wedding? Stop stressing and call us today to book stylish wedding shuttles that will help your big day go off without a hitch. A few sleek 24-passenger minibuses make for fantastic wedding transportation—you can schedule them to swing by your out-of-town guests’ hotels so that nobody has to drive and everyone arrives on time. Because in our book, the bride is the only one allowed to arrive fashionably late to the ceremony!

Church groups also enjoy the minibuses in our network for simple travel to area mission trips and bonding activities. Consolidated group travel makes events feel much more organized, and riding together to service projects is a great way to promote team bonding before the work even begins. Consider renting out an 18-passenger or 20-passenger minibus to make church outings simple and seamless—you’ll thank us later for the suggestion!
