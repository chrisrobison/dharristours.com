Field Trip & School Event Bus Rentals

When it comes to transporting students, colleagues, athletes, team members, friends, faculty, and staff, DHarris Tours will find the perfect transportation option for you.

Why DHarris Tours?

With vehicle options as small as 18-passenger minibuses and as large as 56-passenger full-sized coaches, we’re prepared to accommodate any number of students, staff, faculty, and guests. Whether you’re booking a field trip for a preschool, game-day travel for a high-school, or an off-campus outing for a university, rely on us for safe and comfortable travel accommodations to your next school event. Our dedicated reservation specialists are available 24/7 at 415-902-8542 to answer questions and calculate your free, no-obligation quote!

Safe and Reliable Travel

Buses booked through DHarris Tours are up-to-date on all modern safety standards and are operated only by state-certified, experienced drivers. Relax and enjoy the ride knowing that you and your passengers are in safe hands.

Improved Field Trip Experiences

Ditch the old, uncomfortable school bus on your next field trip. Instead, rent a charter bus and enjoy air conditioning, reclining seats, on-board entertainment, on-board restrooms, and more. You and your students can stay entertained with televisions, DVD players, and WiFi, ensuring the ride to your destination is enjoyable from beginning to end.

Securing your students' and chaperones' comfort is as easy as calling 415-902-8542. Simply let your reservation agent know which amenities will make everyone feel more at home, and we'll go the extra mile to find a bus that ticks every checkbox.

PS: Traveling with a small group? We can still help! A minibus is a great cost-effective alternative to cars.

Easy Off-site Faculty Meetings

Head over to a local restaurant with your colleagues for a faculty meeting, going-away party, or casual get-together. Instead of taking separate cars, stay together on your bus to ensure that everyone arrives together and on time.

Sporting Event Travel Made Simple

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will it cost?

Price may vary depending on location, how long you’ll need the bus, and other factors. For more information, check out our pricing guide or call us at 415-902-8542 for a free, personalized quote.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes— we offer a 10% discount for nonprofit organizations.

Can I book a traditional school bus through DHarris Tours?

No. We only offer charter buses, minibuses, and sometimes small vans. Browse our bus options or call our reservation specialists any time at 415-902-8542 for more information.

Can I wrap the bus with my school’s logo or mascot?

Yes. You’ll just need to reserve one extra day at the beginning of your rental and one extra day at the end to account for the time it will take to wrap and unwrap the bus.

How many buses can I reserve at one time?

You can reserve as many as buses as you need. We’ll put together a fleet that’s perfect for you. 

Do I need to provide food, drinks, or lodging for my driver?

Providing food and drink is not required. You will only need to provide lodging for the driver(s) if your reservation spans multiple days.

How much should I tip my driver?

We recommend tipping your driver(s) 10-20% of the total cost, in cash, if possible.

Are food and drinks allowed on the bus?

Yes. As long as you let your representative know ahead of time, food, drinks, and alcoholic beverages are all allowed on the bus. There is a $250 cleaning fee if a mess is made, however. 

Do you offer smaller, less expensive buses?

Yes. We have a list of buses here, ranging in size from 18-passenger to 56-passenger. We’ll find one that’s perfect for your group.

Do you charge per hour? Is there a minimum rental time?

Rates are charged per hour, with a 5-hour minimum for every trip.

Do I need to find parking for the bus?

No. Our operations team will secure parking and staging areas for the bus prior to your trip.

How often will my driver need to stop for a break?

Break schedules will be negotiated between you and your driver(s) prior to the trip; however, drivers can only legally drive for 10 consecutive hours before they must take an 8-hour required break.

Can I see the bus before my rental is finalized?

Yes. We can send pictures of your bus prior to your date upon request.

What amenities can you offer?

You’ll be able to choose from amenities such as on-board restrooms, televisions with DVD players, plenty of storage space, reclining seats, and more. WiFi and power outlets can be requested at no additional charge. Smaller buses may offer different amenities, and specific amenity packages vary from bus to bus—so be sure to let your rep know what features you'd prefer, so we can track down the perfect bus for your needs! Our reservation specialists can answer any other questions regarding amenities 24/7 at 415-902-8542.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A deposit of up to 30% of the total trip cost will be due after you book your rental.

When will my full balance be due?

Your balance will need to be paid in full 30 days before your trip date.
