FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will it cost?

Price may vary depending on location, how long you’ll need the bus, and other factors. For more information, check out our pricing guide or call us at 415-902-8542 for a free, personalized quote. 

Can I wrap the bus with my company logo?

Yes. You’ll just need to reserve one extra day at the beginning of your rental and one extra day at the end to account for the time it will take to wrap and unwrap the bus. 

Do you offer smaller options, such as sprinter vans?

Yes. Contact our reservation specialists at 415-902-8542 for more details about smaller transportation options.

Can I request an all-black or all-white bus?

Yes. Let our reservation specialists know what type of bus you prefer, and we’ll find the perfect one for you. 

Do you charge per hour? Is there a minimum rental time?

Rates are charged per hour, with a 5-hour minimum for every trip.

How far in advance should I make my reservation?

We recommending reserving your bus 3-6 months advance, if possible, to ensure you’re getting exactly what you’re looking for. However, we are able to provide buses in as little as 2 hours' notice in cities we directly service. 

Can I see the bus before my rental is finalized?

Yes. We can send pictures of your bus prior to your date upon request. 

How many buses can I reserve at one time?

You can reserve as many buses as you need. We’ll put together a fleet that’s perfect for you. 

Can I drink alcohol on the bus?

Yes. Please let your reservation specialist know that you'd like to drink on the bus prior to your trip. Pre-payment may be required and there is a $250 cleaning fee if a mess is left behind. 

How much should I tip my driver?

We recommend tipping your driver(s) 10-20% of the total cost, in cash, if possible.

Do I need to find parking for the bus?

No. Our operations team will secure parking and staging areas for the bus prior to your trip.

How often will my driver need to stop for a break?

Break schedules will be negotiated between you and your driver(s) prior to the trip; however, drivers can only legally drive for 10 consecutive hours before they must take an 8-hour required break.

What amenities can you offer?

You’ll be able to choose from amenities such as on-board restrooms, televisions with DVD players, plenty of storage space, reclining seats, and more. Smaller buses may offer different amenities. WiFi and power outlets can be requested at no additional charge. Our reservation specialists can answer any other questions regarding amenities 24/7 at 415-902-8542.

Do I need to provide food, drinks, or lodging for my driver?

Providing food and drink is not required. You will only need to provide lodging for the driver(s) if your reservation spans multiple days.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A deposit of up to 30% of the total trip cost will be due after you book your rental.

When will my full balance be due?

Your balance will need to be paid in full 30 days before your trip date.
