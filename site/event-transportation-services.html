Corporate Affairs Transportation Made Easy

Planning ground transportation for corporate events can be challenging. With limited parking at corporate venues and high traffic in urban areas, travel options can be limited. Expand your travel range with a transportation method that can cover the full scope of the trip, so important clientele can be transported, picked up, and shuttled to anywhere in the city.

School Field Trip Transportation & Shuttles

Looking to take a school field trip, but not sure how to cut costs? An affordable option when exploring a new city is actually renting with a larger travel group. But are tour buses really the best option when the planned field trip event asks you to meet them at a certain time and destination? Bus tours tend to cost a fortune and typically last the length of several hours, which gets in the way of important field trip plans.

A personal bus driver will be able to quickly drop your school group off at their destination and round all passengers up at your chosen time. With your own private rental, you will be able to store your belongings in the luggage bays safely and securely as you are chauffeured from your school to every educational destination on your itinerary.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an average trip cost?

Pricing can depend on the model and size of the vehicle, the length of the trip, and the location you are shuttling from. For more information, check out our pricing guide or call us at (415) 902-8542 for a free, personalized quote.

What type of buses are available?

The buses in our network include full-sized motorcoaches for large groups like wedding parties and sports teams, along with various models of minibuses for small- to medium-sized groups.

 

What features will my bus come with?

Depending on bus availability and your unique needs, our reservation specialists can pair you with a bus that’s outfitted with comfortable reclining seats, entertainment options, free Wifi, televisions, DVD players, clean restrooms, and more for an enjoyable ride. Be sure to ask your reservation specialist about additional amenities when booking your bus.

Is there a limit on how many buses I can reserve at one time?

There is no limit on the number of buses you can reserve for your event. In order to reserve the best selection of charter buses and minibuses you will need to check availability ahead of time so we can put together a fleet that’s just right for you in the nick of time!

 

Do you charge by hour? Is there a minimum rental time?

Yes, we charge by the hour with a 5-hour minimum. 

How much should I tip my driver?

While tipping is not required, it’s highly appreciated. Our drivers work hard to transport members to your event on your schedule, so if you’re happy with your trip we recommend tipping your driver 10-20% of the total cost.

Do I need to find parking for my bus?

Parking is the responsibility of your bus driver and our operations team. They will secure parking, drop-off, and pickup locations prior to your trip.

Do you offer smaller, less-expensive bus options?

Yes, our bus options range from 18-passengers all the way to 56-passengers.

Can I see the bus before my rental is finalized?

Yes. Upon request, we can send you pictures of your bus prior to your trip.

Will I be required to pay a deposit?

Yes, we require a deposit of 30% of your total trip cost when you book your rental.

When will my full balance be due?

You will be required to pay your balance in full 30 days before your trip date.
