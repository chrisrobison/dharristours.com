Amazing Attractions Await You in San Jose

Most of the city's attractions can be found downtown, where you can also find numerous stores and restaurants, a convention center and beautiful hotels. Your charter bus will allow your group to explore every destination without a worry of navigating on their own, finding parking, or facing traffic. 

Museums: The most important museums are located downtown. The San Jose Museum of Art invites visitors to discover a beautiful collection of modern art, while the Tech Museum of Innovation impresses tourists with its interactive experiences designed for visitors of all ages. Away from the downtown area, you can find the Rosicrucian Egyptian Museum and Planetarium, a fascinating location dedicated to Ancient Egypt.

Landmarks and Gardens: The San Jose City Hall is one of the most admired landmarks in San Jose, a modern building that reflects the innovative style of architects Richard Meier & Partners. You can also visit the Winchester Mystery House, an impressive and odd mansion that seems to be haunted. Don't miss a visit to Santana Row, a European-style village rich in shops, restaurants, and condos, which also houses a farmer's market every Sunday. If you love roses, you will be glad to know that the city houses two beautiful rose gardens: San Jose Municipal Rose Garden and the Heritage Rose Garden.

Amusement Parks: If you have a few days to spend in the city and you've already rented a San Jose charter bus, make sure to include a visit to one (or more) of the city's amusement parks in your itinerary. California's Great America attracts visitors with its roller coasters, thrill rides and plenty of fun things to do for kids, as well as a water park and Las Vegas-style shows. Raging Waters is the perfect place to cool off in the summer, featuring slides, water slides, and a huge wave pool. Golf lovers will surely visit Golfland, which, besides a classic miniature golf course, houses several water slides.

Day Trips and Excursions: Groups who visit San Jose often like to take their bus to surrounding cities, like San Francisco, Sacramento, Portland, and Salt Lake City.
