Check out Top Attractions in & Around Sacramento

Sacramento is home to some impressive attractions and has easy access to nearby destinations like San Francisco, Fresno, Bakersfield, Modesto, and San Jose.

Museums and Landmarks: The State Indian Museum houses interesting exhibits about the traditions of the Indian tribes of California. Visit the Crocker Art Museum to discover a large collection of California art, and dedicate some time to the California State Capitol, which includes Capitol Park. If your group wants to see the oldest restored fort in the US, head to Sutter's Fort. It was built in the 1840s and houses a fascinating collection of California artifacts.

Outdoor Fun: For outdoor fun, be sure to visit Cesar Chavez Park, a beautiful park located right in the city center, close to Sacramento Public Library. Folsom Lake is one of the largest lakes in Northern California and is the perfect destination for fishing, kayaking, biking, and sailing. Spend a fun family day at the Sacramento Zoo and the Fairy Tale Town.
