Serving Locations All Across North America!

DHarris Charters is your go-to source for booking a charter bus wherever you are located. We serve every major market in the United States and our location base is continuously expanding. We have access to thousands of modern buses. 

We can provide you with quality charter bus services to any location (via land) in the United States and parts of Canada. If you are unsure about your service area, give us a call at any time. Our reservation agents are available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year and will be able to provide you with answers to any question and get you an instant quote. Whether you have a party of 10 or a party of 10,000, the DHarris team will form a customized itinerary specific to your transportation needs.

We’re happy to help groups looking for ongoing shuttles, too. Do you need a week of shuttle services for a major convention or an easy way to get your employees from the train station to work everyday? Just email our long-term contracts specialist at shuttles@dharristours.com or call (415) 902-8542 and we’ll find a safe, thoroughly inspected charter bus or minibus for your organization.
