School Event Charter Bus & Minibus Rental

Charter Buses for All Bay Area School Events

Planning transportation for a school function? San Francisco Charter Bus Company is equipped to handle all of your group travel logistics, whether you’re in need of a compact minibus or a full-sized charter bus. We’ve helped everyone from elementary schools to universities organize transportation for a variety of educational events. Call us today at 415-404-6456 to learn more about how we can help with your next school trip!

Comfortable and Safe Travels

We charter buses equipped with amenities such as plush, reclining seats, air conditioning, WiFi, extra storage space, and even on-board restrooms on larger models. Your students and chaperones will all enjoy a comfortable trip on a modern charter bus.

You’ll also travel stress-free with your professional driver who will handle the operation and navigation of your bus on the way to your destination. Relax and enjoy your ride when you book with us!

School Sports Travel Made Simple

Need a bus for an away game? With a variety of bus sizes available, we’ll make sure your fleet perfectly accommodates your team, your staff, and your coaches. Ride in comfort with reclining seats and televisions with DVD players all the way to your next big game. Your team will be rested and ready to play when you arrive.University Outings with a Private Bus

Whether it’s a greek event or a day trip with friends, a private bus is a great way to travel with classmates. Book a minibus for a short outing or a full-sized coach for a long trip. We’ll make sure your driver gets you safely back to campus when your trip is finished.
